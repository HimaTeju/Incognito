About College

Established in 1973 under the banner of the prestigious Seshadripuram Educational Trust, Seshadripuram College regularly features among the top Commerce Colleges in the Country and has secured the grade 'A' in the NAAC accreditation, with more than 3000 UG and PG students. The college attempts to create a harmonious teaching and learning environment that equips students with the knowledge and skills required to face the demands of the globalized environment without sacrificing the core values of our culture rooted in righteousness. It attempts to live up to its stated mission of nurturing students who are intellectually alive, morally upright and socially responsible.


About BCA

The BCA Course commenced in our college in the year 2013 with a sanctioned intake of 30 students per batch. In 9 years, it has grown to two sections per batch with as sanctioned intake of 180 students. The Department of Computer Science is fully equipped with latest systems and Wi-Fi enabled labs. We have an active placement cell which has placed 1/3rd of the first outgoing batch in to reputed companies like Wipro, Infosys, and TCS etc. The Department of Computer Science also offers various advanced Add-on courses which equip students to face current industry requirements.


About MCA

Seshadripuram College, Bangalore, offer MCA course affiliated to Bangalore City University under AICTE regulations in 2024. The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) is a two-years program designed for students to flexibly adapt, consistently adhere to principles, effectively apply their skills, and strive for a harmonious balance and excellence in their careers. Through this empowerment, they are enabled to contribute significant value to both industry and society.
