INCOGNITO

About Incognito

Incognito is the annual inter collegiate IT Fest organised by The Department of Computer Science. The programme has remained the instituition's pride event since 2015. This event aims to focus on advancing the practical and skill based learning initiative to promote competitive spirit among its participants. The event further promotes the innovative programming skills among the young minds
