SINGING

Rules & Regulations

  • Minimum 3 and maximum 8 performers are allowed in group singing.
  • Duration is 3+1 minutes .
  • Singing is permitted in kannada ,English, Hindi.
  • Rap songs are prohibited.
  • Participants using karaoke are instructed to submit the same during registration.
  • Participants should carry their own instruments ,if required.
  • Usage of indecent language/lyrics is strictly prohibited.
  • Violation of above rules leads to disqualification.
  • Judge's decision is final.
